Animals

Title:Pig

Description:

The pig is an intelligent, social, omnivorous mammal with a stout body, short legs, and a distinctive snout for rooting.

Title:Bear

Description:

Bears are large, intelligent omnivores with stocky legs, a strong sense of smell, and non-retractable claws, found across the Americas and Eurasia.

Title:Tiger

Description:

The tiger is the largest cat species, an apex predator known for its unique black stripes and being a solitary, powerful hunter and excellent swimmer.
